Peepur Rat Clan

    About

    The Peepur Rat Clan originated from the Rattus population of Atlantis. When Bartholomew V (Clone of Bartholomew IV) took over as king of Atlantis, he attempted to execute all Ratti, in an effort to make all living Atlantians Bartholinians. The remaining few Ratti (approx. 3) emigrated from Atlantis to found their own country, the Peepur Rat Clan. Years later, with a strong army, they set out to gather Radioactive Honey to dip their soldiers in. This began the Great Honey War that lasted for many years. By the end of the war, they had gathered enough Radioactive Honey to create a new, far stronger army. They dipped their soldiers into the Radioactive Honey to create Peepur Honey Rat, their ultimate warrior. As fast as sound, and as strong as a strongman, the Peepur Honey Rat is a formidable foe.

    Current State

    Population: 20 Million, and growing
    Community Health: Healthy
    Security: Extremely safe, with little to no crime due to the Peepur Honey Rats.
    Alignment: Greater Good (Will commit war crimes if it means a better future)
