Bartholomew Bartholomore IV

    About

    Bartholomew is everyone's favorite dude. He is a rainbow-ish colored octopus whose full name is Bartholomew Bartholomore IV. When Bartholomew IV was the monarch of Atlantis, he was such a good king that scientists were researching how to clone Bartholomew IV. After many failed attempts (and creating an entirely new species known as the Bartholinian), the cloning succeeded and created a new king to rule after Bartholomew IV's untimely death. Bartholomew Bartholomore IV is still alive and well. His death was merely fabricated by the Bartholinians, as they wanted their own kind to be the ruling species.

    Current State

    Currently (as of 2023), Bartholomew resides in Alaska, and is quite healthy.

    Background

    Bartholomew was born in Poland, however human children tormented him so much that he moved to Atlantis, where he cooked and sold pierogis for a living. Eventually, he discovered his true meaning in life when a royal scientist named Squidward sent him a letter informing him that he contains more royal blood than the current king of Atlantis, meaning Bartholomew was the true king.
